How Often Should You Service Your AC? (India Guide 2025)

By Air Control Experts  |  Updated April 2025  |  7 min read

AK
Ajay Kumar Senior HVAC Engineer, Air Control (est. 1987) · 38 years AC repair & HVAC experience across Delhi NCR · Served 15+ embassies & Fortune 500 campuses
✅ Quick Answer

AC service frequency in Delhi NCR: Clean the filter every 3–4 weeks during summer. Get a professional service (coil clean + drain flush + gas check) once before summer (March–April). Get a deep chemical wash once per year. Delhi's extreme dust clogs ACs 3–5× faster than cleaner cities — skipping service increases electricity bills by 15–25% and leads to expensive repairs.

Most people service their AC only when it breaks down — which is exactly the wrong time. By then, dirt buildup has already caused coil corrosion, the compressor has been working 20% harder for months, and what would have been a ₹700 service call has become a ₹5,000 repair bill. This guide gives you a science-backed, India-specific schedule for AC maintenance that maximises cooling performance, minimises electricity bills, and extends your AC's life.

Why Delhi NCR ACs Need More Frequent Service Than Other Places

Delhi's air quality is among the worst in the world. Dust, construction debris, PM2.5 particles, and industrial pollutants clog AC filters 3–5× faster than a clean-air city. In summer (May–August), an AC in Delhi running 12 hours daily accumulates the same amount of filter dust in 3 weeks that would take 3 months in a clean environment. Add Noida's industrial air and Gurgaon's construction zones, and you understand why Delhi NCR ACs need more attention.

The Recommended AC Service Schedule for Delhi NCR

Every 3–4 Weeks (During Summer): Filter Cleaning — DIY

The air filter is your first line of defence. A clogged filter restricts airflow, causes ice formation, and forces the compressor to work harder. During peak summer when your AC runs 10–16 hours daily, clean the filter every 3–4 weeks. This is a simple DIY task: remove the front panel, slide out the filter, wash under running water, dry fully, and reinstall.

Once Before Summer (March–April): Basic Annual Service

Before summer's peak, get a professional service: foam or chemical coil clean, drain pipe flush, refrigerant pressure check, electrical inspection. This ensures your AC is ready for the brutal June–August heat. Booking in March–April avoids the May–June rush when technician availability is tight.

Once a Year: Deep Chemical Wash

A deep chemical wash uses special coil cleaning chemicals to dissolve scale, mould, bacteria, and oil deposits that foam washing can't remove. Recommended once annually for ACs running 8+ hours daily. If your AC has a musty smell when it starts, a chemical wash + disinfection is the cure.

Every 2 Years (or If AC Is 5+ Years Old): Full Inspection

Older ACs need a comprehensive check: copper pipe condition, flare connection integrity, capacitor test, compressor current draw measurement, thermostat calibration, and outdoor unit condition. This catches developing problems before they become expensive failures.

After Any Power Cut or Voltage Fluctuation: Quick Check

Delhi NCR has frequent power cuts and voltage fluctuations that can damage capacitors, PCBs, and inverter boards. After a major power event, test your AC and check for error codes. If it trips or shows errors, call a technician before running it for extended periods.

What's Included in a Professional AC Service?

  • Air filter cleaning or replacement
  • Evaporator coil cleaning — foam wash (basic) or chemical wash (deep)
  • Condenser coil cleaning — outdoor unit coil wash
  • Drain pipe flushing — clears algae and blockages that cause water leaks
  • Refrigerant pressure check — confirms no gas has leaked
  • Electrical connections check — tightens loose terminals, checks for corrosion
  • Thermostat / temperature sensor check
  • Overall performance test — confirms AC reaches set temperature

What's NOT Included (Extra Charges)

  • Gas refilling (if low) — ₹2,000–₹4,500 depending on gas type
  • PCB repair or replacement
  • Capacitor replacement
  • Drain pipe replacement
  • Coil repair or replacement
Tip: An AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) bundles 2 services per year + free emergency call-outs into one annual fee. For a single 1.5-ton split AC, an Air Control AMC costs less than the price of one emergency repair call. See our AMC plans.

Signs Your AC Needs Immediate Service (Don't Wait)

  • Musty or mouldy smell when AC starts — mould in the drain pan or coil
  • Water dripping from the indoor unit inside the room — blocked drain pipe
  • Weak airflow despite fan running at high speed — filter or coil blocked
  • AC not reaching set temperature even after 1 hour — coil dirty or gas low
  • Rattling or squealing noise — debris in fan or fan bearing worn
  • Electricity bill 20%+ higher than same month last year — efficiency loss
  • Ice forming on the pipe or unit — filter/coil blocked or gas low
  • Error codes on display — immediate attention needed

How Service Frequency Affects Electricity Bills

Research shows that dirty evaporator coils reduce AC efficiency by 5–25% depending on severity. For a 1.5-ton AC running 10 hours daily at ₹8/kWh, a 15% efficiency loss adds approximately ₹1,800–₹2,400 to your summer electricity bill. A ₹700 annual service pays for itself many times over in electricity savings alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my AC in India?
For Delhi NCR conditions: clean the filter every 3-4 weeks during summer; get a basic service (filter + coil clean + drain check) once before summer (March-April); get a deep chemical wash once a year; get a full inspection every 2 years or if the AC is 5+ years old.
Can I service my AC myself?
You can safely clean the filter yourself — remove the front panel, slide out the filter, wash under running water, dry, and reinstall. All other servicing (coil cleaning, gas check, drain cleaning, electrical inspection) should be done by a certified technician.
What is included in AC service?
A standard AC service includes: air filter cleaning, evaporator coil cleaning (foam or chemical wash), condenser coil cleaning, drain pipe clearing, refrigerant pressure check, electrical connection inspection, thermostat check, and overall performance assessment.
How do I know my AC needs servicing?
Signs your AC needs immediate service: reduced cooling efficiency, bad smell when AC starts (musty/mouldy), weak airflow, water dripping from indoor unit, unusual noise, visible dirt on the filter, and electricity bills higher than usual.
How much does AC service cost in Delhi NCR?
AC service costs: basic service ₹400–₹700; deep chemical wash ₹800–₹1,500; service + gas top-up ₹2,000–₹4,000. An AMC covering 2 services plus emergency calls typically costs ₹2,500–₹5,000 per unit per year.
When is the best time to service AC in Delhi?
The best time is March or early April — before the summer heat peak. This ensures your AC is ready for June-September when it runs 12-16 hours daily. Avoid the May-June rush when technician availability is limited.
