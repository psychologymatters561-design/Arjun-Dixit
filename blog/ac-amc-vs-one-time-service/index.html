AC AMC vs One-Time Service: Which Is Better for You?

By Air Control Experts  |  Updated April 2025  |  7 min read

The age-old question for Delhi NCR homeowners: pay for an AC AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) or just call a technician when something goes wrong? Both approaches have their place. This guide breaks down the real costs, benefits, and situations where each makes financial sense — with actual numbers from Delhi NCR pricing.

What Is an AC AMC?

An AC Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a pre-paid service agreement where you pay once per year and receive scheduled services + priority emergency support. For a 1.5-ton split AC, a typical Air Control AMC includes:

  • 2 scheduled professional services per year (pre-summer + mid-season)
  • Unlimited emergency call-outs at no extra visit charge
  • Priority response (typically within 4–8 hours vs 1–3 days without AMC)
  • Filter cleaning at every visit
  • Refrigerant pressure check at every service
  • Full performance report

Head-to-Head Comparison

🔵 AC AMC (Annual Contract)

  • ✅ Fixed annual cost — no surprises
  • ✅ 2 scheduled services included
  • ✅ Emergency visits at no extra call charge
  • ✅ Priority response time
  • ✅ Same technician builds familiarity with your units
  • ✅ Preventive checks catch problems early
  • ❌ Upfront cost even if no breakdowns occur
  • ❌ Parts not always included (non-comprehensive)

🟠 Pay Per Visit (One-Time Service)

  • ✅ No commitment — pay only when needed
  • ✅ Good for new ACs under warranty
  • ✅ Good if usage is very low (<4 hrs/day)
  • ❌ Higher per-visit cost
  • ❌ No priority — long waits in peak summer
  • ❌ Easy to skip services and let problems develop
  • ❌ Each technician starts fresh — no history
  • ❌ Emergency calls billed at premium rates

Comprehensive vs Non-Comprehensive AMC

There are two types of AMC — and the difference matters a lot:

  • Non-Comprehensive AMC: Covers service visits and labour. Parts (capacitors, filters, PCBs, etc.) billed separately if needed. Lower annual cost.
  • Comprehensive AMC: Covers everything including parts (except gas refilling, which is usually a separate charge). Higher annual cost but complete peace of mind.

For newer ACs (0–4 years, often under manufacturer warranty), a non-comprehensive AMC makes sense — the probability of parts failure is low. For older ACs (5+ years), a comprehensive AMC is usually worth the extra cost.

The Verdict

Choose AMC if: Your AC is 3+ years old, you use it 8+ hours daily in summer, you have multiple AC units, you live or work in an area with dusty air, or you've had breakdowns in previous summers.

Choose Pay-Per-Visit if: Your AC is brand new (0–2 years) and under manufacturer warranty, you use it less than 4 hours daily, or it's a second/guest room AC used only occasionally.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between AC AMC and one-time service?
An AC AMC is a pre-paid annual plan that includes 2 scheduled services, unlimited emergency visit calls, and priority response. One-time service means you pay per visit as needed. AMC is better if you use your AC heavily (8+ hours/day) or have older units prone to breakdowns.
Is AC AMC worth it in India?
Yes, for most households in Delhi NCR. A single emergency repair call can cost ₹1,500–₹5,000. An AMC plan covering 2 services + emergency calls costs ₹2,500–₹5,000 annually. If you have even 1 emergency repair per year, AMC pays for itself. For newer ACs (0-3 years), one-time service may be more economical.
What is a comprehensive vs non-comprehensive AMC?
A comprehensive AMC covers both service (labour) AND parts — if something fails and needs replacement, the parts cost is included. A non-comprehensive AMC covers service visits and labour only. Comprehensive AMC costs more but gives complete peace of mind.
What does AC AMC not cover?
Typical AC AMC exclusions: refrigerant (gas) refilling charges, physical damage from misuse, damage from electrical surges or lightning, compressor replacement on older units, cosmetic damage to panels. Always read the AMC terms carefully.
Can I get AMC for an old AC?
Yes, but most AMC providers will inspect the AC first before issuing an AMC on older units (5+ years). Air Control offers AMC for ACs of all ages after a paid inspection.
