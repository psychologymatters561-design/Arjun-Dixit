Common Issues in Delhi

AC Service Problems We Solve in Delhi

🌡️

Reduced Cooling

AC not as cold as before — dirty coil reducing heat exchange efficiency.

😷

Bad/Musty Smell

Foul odour from vents — mold/algae on evaporator coil.

💨

Weak Airflow

Less air than usual — clogged filter or dirty blower wheel.

💧

Water Leaking

Dripping from indoor unit — blocked drain from dust buildup.

⚡

High Electricity Bill

Dirty coil makes compressor work harder — using more power.

❄️

Low Gas / Not Enough Cooling

Gas level low — needs checking and topping up.

Deep Chemical Wash
