Common Issues in Gurgaon

AC Repair Problems We Solve in Gurgaon

❄️

AC Not Cooling

Room stays hot even with AC running — low refrigerant, dirty coil, or compressor issue.

🌡️

AC Blowing Hot Air

Warm air from vents — gas leak, compressor not running, or reversing valve fault.

💧

Water Leaking

Dripping from indoor unit — blocked drain pipe or frozen and thawing coil.

🔊

Unusual Noises

Rattling, squealing, banging — loose parts, fan blade issue, or compressor bearing.

⚡

Tripping MCB

AC trips circuit breaker — capacitor, PCB short, or compressor overload.

📟

AC Not Starting

No power-on — PCB fault, fuse blown, or power supply issue.

🧊

Ice on Unit

Frost/ice on indoor unit — severely low gas or complete airflow blockage.

📶

Error Code Blinking

Fault code on display — sensor, PCB, or communication error.
