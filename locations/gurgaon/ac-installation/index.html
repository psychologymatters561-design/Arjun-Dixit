Common Issues in Gurgaon

AC Installation Problems We Solve in Gurgaon

📐

Wrong AC Placement

Incorrectly placed ACs cool unevenly — we advise on the best location before installation.

🔗

Poor Copper Piping

Cheap aluminium pipes or uninsulated lines cause gas loss and efficiency drop.

⚡

No Dedicated Electrical Point

AC sharing sockets with other appliances causes tripping.

❄️

Wrong AC Size

Undersized AC never cools the room; oversized AC short-cycles and wastes power.

💧

Poor Drainage Planning

Drain pipe not planned properly leads to water dripping on walls.

🔧

Improper Outdoor Unit Placement

Boxed-in outdoor unit overheats — kills compressor in 1-2 seasons.
