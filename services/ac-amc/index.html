AC AMC FAQs — Delhi NCR

An AC AMC is a yearly service agreement where we visit your AC units on a scheduled basis for preventive maintenance — cleaning, gas checking, and inspection — and also provide priority support for any breakdown calls during the contract period.

A comprehensive AMC covers both labour AND spare parts (within agreed limits). A non-comprehensive AMC covers only labour and service visits — parts like compressors or PCBs are charged separately. We offer both options.

AC AMC cost depends on the number of units and type: non-comprehensive AMC for a single split AC starts at ₹1,800–₹2,500/year. Comprehensive AMC is ₹3,500–₹6,000/year per unit. Multi-unit discounts apply for homes and offices with 3+ ACs.

Our standard AMC includes 2–4 scheduled service visits per year plus unlimited emergency call-outs (with a response guarantee). Exact visits depend on the plan selected.
