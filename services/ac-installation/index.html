What We Install

AC Types We Install

🏠

Split AC

Wall-mounted indoor + outdoor unit. Most popular for homes and offices. 1 ton, 1.5 ton, 2 ton. All brands.

🪟

Window AC

Single unit fitted in a wall or window opening. Common in older buildings and shops.

🔲

Cassette AC

Ceiling-flush 4-way airflow cassette unit. Ideal for large rooms, restaurants, and open-plan offices.

🌀

Ceiling / Floor Mount

High-capacity ceiling or floor-mounted units for server rooms, large halls, and commercial spaces.

🏢

Ducted AC Systems

Concealed ducted systems for villas, boutique hotels, and premium offices. Central air through ducts.

❄️

VRF / VRV Systems

Multi-zone VRF systems for large commercial spaces. One outdoor unit, multiple indoor units.

Installation Process

Our AC Installation Steps

📐

1. Site Survey

We assess wall type, room layout, outdoor space, and electrical capacity before beginning.

🔩

2. Bracket & Mounting

Heavy-duty outdoor bracket installed. Indoor unit mounted at correct height and angle for optimal airflow.

🔗

3. Copper Piping

Correct diameter copper pipe with proper insulation. No cheap aluminium pipes. Precision brazing at joints.

❄️

4. Gas Charging

System evacuated and charged with correct refrigerant (R-32 / R-410A). Pressure tested before start-up.

FAQs

AC Installation FAQs — Delhi NCR

Split AC installation in Delhi NCR typically costs ₹1,200–₹2,500 depending on the unit type and copper pipe length required. 1-ton/1.5-ton split AC standard installation starts at ₹1,200. Prices include indoor unit mounting, outdoor unit bracket, 3m copper pipe, and gas charging.

A standard split AC installation takes 2–4 hours. Cassette or ceiling-mount AC may take 4–6 hours. Ducted systems can take 1–3 days depending on complexity.

The indoor unit should be installed on an exterior wall (or near one), at least 7–8 feet high, away from direct sunlight, and with clear airflow across the room. Our technicians survey the room and recommend the optimal position.

The outdoor unit needs ventilation — minimum 30cm clearance on all sides. It should not be in direct sunlight if possible, and must be accessible for maintenance. It must never be boxed in. Our team selects the best location.

Yes. We install Daikin, Voltas, LG, Samsung, Carrier, Blue Star, Hitachi, Panasonic, O'General, Godrej, Whirlpool, Haier, Mitsubishi and all other brands available in India.

Room size guidelines: up to 100 sq ft = 0.75–1 ton, 100–150 sq ft = 1–1.5 ton, 150–200 sq ft = 1.5–2 ton. Heat load, ceiling height, sunlight exposure, and number of occupants also affect the right capacity. We calculate this for you.

Yes. We install all types: split AC, window AC, cassette AC, ceiling-mounted AC, tower AC, and ducted AC systems for homes, offices, and commercial spaces.

Standard installation includes 3 metres of copper pipe. Additional pipe is charged extra per metre. We always use proper insulation on copper lines to prevent condensation and energy loss.

Yes. AC requires a dedicated 15A or 16A electrical point. Do not share with other appliances. We check electrical capacity and can advise on wiring if needed — though electrical work beyond the AC point is done by licensed electricians.

Absolutely. Many customers buy AC from Amazon, Flipkart, or brand showrooms and hire us for installation. We bring all materials — bracket, copper pipe, insulation, drain pipe — and complete the full installation.
