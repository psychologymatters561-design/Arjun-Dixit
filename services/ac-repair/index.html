Common Problems

AC Problems We Fix — All Types, All Brands

We repair every AC problem — from simple gas top-ups to complex compressor replacements. Daikin, Voltas, LG, Samsung, Carrier, Blue Star, Hitachi, Panasonic, O'General and all brands.

❄️

AC Not Cooling

AC running but room temperature stays high. Caused by low gas, dirty coils, or compressor issues.

🌡️

AC Blowing Hot Air

Warm air from vents — usually low refrigerant, compressor fault, or reversing valve stuck.

💧

Water Leaking / Dripping

Water dripping from indoor unit — blocked drain pipe, frozen coil, or dirty filter.

🔊

Unusual Noises

Rattling, squealing, banging — loose parts, fan blade, or failing compressor bearing.

⚡

AC Tripping Electricity

MCB trips when AC runs — capacitor failure, compressor overload, or PCB short.

🧊

Ice Forming on Unit

Ice/frost on indoor unit — severely low refrigerant or blocked airflow.

📟

AC Not Starting

AC doesn't power on — PCB fault, capacitor dead, or electrical supply issue.

💨

Weak Airflow

Barely any air from vents — clogged filter, dirty blower wheel, or fan motor weak.

🔴

Error Codes Blinking

Error light/code on display — diagnostic fault, sensor failure, or PCB error.

🌡️

AC Not Reaching Set Temperature

AC running but never hitting set temp — undersized unit, gas low, or poor insulation.

AC Repair FAQs — Delhi NCR

AC repair cost in Delhi NCR typically ranges from ₹300–₹2,500 depending on the fault. Gas refilling costs ₹800–₹2,000, PCB repair ₹1,200–₹3,500, compressor repair ₹3,000–₹8,000. Air Control provides transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

If your AC is not cooling after service, the most likely causes are: low refrigerant gas (needs refilling), dirty evaporator coil, faulty compressor, or a blocked air filter. Our technician will diagnose the exact issue before any repair.

AC blowing warm air usually means refrigerant gas is low, compressor is not running, or the reversing valve is stuck. Call us for same-day diagnosis.

Rattling sounds from an AC usually indicate loose fan blades, debris inside the unit, or a worn-out motor bearing. It can also signal a failing compressor. Get it checked immediately to avoid bigger damage.

Water dripping from the indoor unit is caused by a clogged drain pipe, dirty air filter blocking airflow, or low gas causing the coil to freeze and then thaw. We clear drain lines and identify the root cause.

Yes. We offer emergency AC repair across Delhi NCR with a 2-hour response guarantee for critical failures. Call +91 93122 64832 any time.

AC tripping the MCB is caused by an electrical short, overloaded compressor, capacitor failure, or PCB fault. This is a safety issue — do not keep resetting the MCB. Call a technician immediately.

In most cases a non-responsive remote means dead batteries or IR sensor issues. But if the AC doesn't respond even to the physical panel buttons, there may be a PCB or power supply fault.

Most standard repairs (gas filling, coil cleaning, minor electrical) are completed in 1–3 hours on-site. Complex repairs like compressor replacement may take 1–2 days if parts need to be sourced.
