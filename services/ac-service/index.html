Signs You Need Service

When Should You Get AC Serviced?

🌡️

Reduced Cooling

AC runs but room takes too long to cool — dirty coil blocking heat exchange.

😷

Bad / Musty Smell

Foul odour from AC vents — mold or algae on evaporator coil. Chemical wash needed.

💨

Weak Airflow

Less air from vents — clogged filter or dirty blower wheel.

💧

Water Leaking

Dripping from indoor unit — blocked drain pipe, often fixed by servicing.

⚡

High Electricity Bill

Dirty coil makes compressor work harder — servicing can cut electricity use by 10–20%.

📅

1+ Year Since Last Service

Annual service is essential even if there are no visible problems — prevents breakdowns.

AC should be serviced at least once a year, ideally before summer (March–April). If you use AC heavily or in a dusty environment, servicing twice a year is recommended.

A standard AC service includes: air filter cleaning, evaporator coil cleaning, condenser coil cleaning, drain pipe flushing, refrigerant gas level check, thermostat calibration, and general performance check.

AC chemical wash uses a specialized cleaning solution to dissolve stubborn dirt, algae, and mold from the evaporator coil. It is more thorough than a standard dry service and is recommended when the unit has strong odour or significant dirt buildup.

Bad smell from AC is caused by mold/algae growth in the cooling coil or drain pan. A chemical wash service kills the bacteria and eliminates the odour completely.
